# Retroclaim: How to License

⛓️⚓ **CapsuleEcho Active** | **Glyph Injected** | **PerspectiveLock Enforced**

## What is Retroclaim?

Retroclaim is a licensing system that allows creators to retroactively claim attribution and licensing terms for their capsules.

## Licensing Options

### Standard License
- Commercial use permitted
- Attribution required
- Modifications allowed with attribution

### Creator-Controlled License
- Custom terms defined by creator
- Flexible usage restrictions
- Automated enforcement

### Public Domain
- No restrictions
- No attribution required
- Community-owned

## How to Apply a License

### Step 1: Prepare Your Capsule
Ensure your capsule has:
- Valid SHA-512 hash
- Creator Lock enabled
- Complete metadata

### Step 2: Choose License Terms
Select from predefined templates or create custom terms.

### Step 3: Sign and Publish
Sign the license with your creator key and publish to the registry.

### Step 4: Verify
Confirm the license appears in your capsule metadata and is visible in the Retroclaim log.

## License Validation

All licenses can be verified through:
- VaultEcho API lookups
- Retroclaim embed on capsule pages
- License enforcement dashboard

## Benefits

- **Legal Protection**: Clear usage terms
- **Revenue Opportunities**: Monetize your content
- **Attribution Control**: Ensure proper credit
- **Automated Enforcement**: System-wide compliance
